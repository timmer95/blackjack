# User Story - Place Bets

## Story
As a HumanPlayer, I want to enter a bet amount, so that I can play.

## Acceptance criteria
Program asks for user input
After user input, the amount is stored in Player's bet
A Player cannot place a bet that is is higher than their cash
A Player cannot place a bet that is (upon multiplication with 3:2) is higher than their cash
Multiple players acn input their bet

# User Story - Transfer Bet Money

## Story
As a Player, I want to receive and pay bet money, so that I can play bets.

## Acceptance criteria
Upon winning, the Dealer receives money from the HumanPlayer
Upon losing, the Dealer pays the HumanPlayer's Bet to the HumanPlayer
Upon winning with BlackJack, the Dealer receives 3:2 ration money from the HumanPlayer
Upon losing with BlackJack, the Dealer pays the HumanPlayer's Bet with 3:2 ratio to the HumanPlayer
